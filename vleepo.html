Vleepo


Vleepo is a new social-networking mobile application that redefines what group chat can be. User could create a “vleepo” as a customizable canvas that allows users to group chat, blog, host events, share media contents, and build communities on the platform. It pulls people, activities and content closer together while providing relevant vleepos based on interests, friends, location.

Brief

Our team worked through potential solutions to improve the overall user experience by adopting an agile design sprint approach. We focused on “Discover” feature where merging discover vleepos and discover people into on Discover tab, allowing users to look for relevant vleepos and new people in one place.
